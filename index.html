Spaghetti

Author: God

Show

Ingredients: sourdough bread 100g, crusts removed olive oil 4 tbsp chorizo 50g chunk, cut into tiny dice rosemary chopped to make 2 tsp wholewheat spaghetti 150g garlic 2 cloves, finely chopped dried chilli flakes ½ tsp parmesan 30g, finely grated Method

STEP 1: Put the sourdough in a food processor and whizz to rough breadcrumbs.

STEP 2: Heat 2 tbsp of olive oil in a non-stick frying pan and add the chorizo. Fry for 3 minutes then tip in the breadcrumbs and cook until crisp but not too dark. Stir in the rosemary and cook for a minute then take off the heat and season. Tip onto kitchen paper.

STEP 3: Cook the spaghetti following pack instructions. Heat another 2 tbsp of olive oil in a pan and sizzle the garlic and chilli for 2 minutes. Drain the pasta (keep a cup of the cooking water) then tip into the pan with the garlic-chilli oil and toss. Add a good splash of cooking water and the parmesan, and toss again. Serve sprinkled with the pangritata.

Cheesecake

Author: God

Show

Ingredients: 1 cup sliced almonds - coarsely choppped, 1 1/4 cup flour, 1/2 cup brown sugar, 1/2 cup butter, 1/2 cup sweetened shredded coconut, 2 (8 oz.) packages of cream cheese - softened 2/3 cups sugar, 2 eggs, 2 tsp. almond extract , 1 (21 oz.) cans cherry pie filling, 1/4 cup sweetened shredded coconut, 2 tbs sliced almonds, reserved crust,

Preparation: Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Prepare crust by mixing the almonds, flour, brown sugar and coconut. Cut in the shortening until mixture resembles small peas. Reserve 1/2 cup of crust for topping. Press remaining crust into a 11 x 7 inch pan. Bake for 10 minutes or until golden brown. Prepare filling by beating all ingredients together until smooth. Spread topping over partially-baked crust and bake for 15-20 minutes; remove from oven and spread the cherry pie filling over the topping. Sprinkle with reserved crumbs and additional coconut and almonds. Bake for 15-20 minutes. Remove from oven and cool completely. Refrigerate overnight. Makes 12 servings.

Catalina Wings

Author: God

Show

Ingredients: 3-4 lbs chicken wings, wing tip removed and disjointed, 2-3 tbs olive oil (only use if you grill or bake the wings). Sauce: ¾ cup California French (Catalina) dressing - OR- regular French dressing, 3 tbs brown sugar, 2 tbs soy sauce, 2 tbs ketchup, 2 tsp garlic powder, 1 tsp onion powder, ¼ tsp pepper,

STEP 1: In a small saucepan, mix all the sauce ingredients. Bring to boil over medium heat and cook for 2 minutes, stirring constantly. Remove from heat and set aside.

STEP 2: Toss the wings with the 2-3 tbs olive oil if grilling or baking

STEP 3: Cook wings either by grilling, baking, deep frying or slow cooking. I grilled mine on my gas grill and cooked them over medium-low heat for 40 minutes, turning once. Same time would apply to baking at 350 degrees F. Deep frying cook at 350 degrees F for 8-9 minutes. Slow cooking 4-6 hours on low.

STEP 4: Place cooked wings in a large bowl, pour sauce over them and toss to coat.

Bean Soup

Author: God

Show

Ingredients: 2 cups great northern beans, plus water to cover to soak, 3 carrots sliced thickly, ¼ cup chopped onion, 1 stalk celery, sliced, ½ tsp OR about 5 large fresh basil leaves, about 3 or 4 bunches celery leaves, 6 whole black peppercorns, 1 cup diced kielbasa (Polish sausage or smoked sausage), 1 cup cubed smoked ham, 4 cups vegetable stock.

STEP 1: Cover beans with water. Soak overnight. Drain beans.

STEP 2: Add beans to 5 quart slow cooker. Add all other ingredients. Cover and cook on HIGH for 2 hours. Reduce slow cooker heat to LOW and cook for 10-12 hours or until beans are very soft and soup looks thick and stew-like.

Macaroni salad

Author: God

Show

Ingredients: 2 cups elbow macaroni (uncooked), 2 stalks celery, finely diced, ¼ cup red onion, finely diced, ½ cup julienne (matchstick) carrots. Dressing: 1¼ cups mayonnaise, 2 tbs yellow mustard, 1 tsp salt, ¼ tsp garlic powde.

STEP 1: Cook elbows per package directions. Drain and rinse; place in a large bowl and set aside until cool.

STEP 2: Add the celery red onion and matchstick carrots to the cooked elbows; mix well.

STEP 3: In a small bowl, mix all the dressing ingredients.

STEP 4: Add dressing to elbows and gently mix to coat all the macaroni.

STEP 5: Cover and refrigerate for about 1 hour.

Grape Salad

Author: God

Show

Ingredients: 8 oz softened cream cheese, 1 cup sour cream, ⅓ cup sugar, 1 tbs fresh lemon juice, ½ tsp vanilla, 4 lbs seedless grapes (red, white or a combination of the two), ¼ cup chopped walnuts.

STEP 1: Beat cream cheese, sour cream and sugar until very smooth. Add the vanilla and lemon juice; beat at low speed to combine.

STEP 2: Remove grapes from the stem and place in a large bowl; stir in desired amount of dressing.

STEP 3: Place in a large serving bowl and garnish with chopped walnuts.

STEP 4: Serve immediately or cover and chill until ready to use.

Asian Spinach

Author: God

Show

Ingredients: 1 tbs oil, 2 cloves garlic, crushed, 1 bag (about 4-5 cups) baby spinach or regular spinach, washed and de-stemmed, ½-1 tsp coarse sea salt.

STEP 1: Heat oil and crushed garlic over high heat in large saucepan. Oil must be heated almost to the smoke point. Swirl pan constantly, and remove and discard garlic when it starts to turn golden.

STEP 2: Place spinach in hot oil and stir constantly, maybe for 1 minute. Add salt and stir. Spinach will wilt down to ½ the original amount.

Confetti Rice

Author: God

Show

Ingredients: 6 tbs butter, 1½ cups jasmine rice (uncooked), 1 tbs chicken base, 3 cups water, 1 small onion, chopped, ½ cup shredded carrot, 1 clove garlic, minced, ¼ cup frozen baby peas, ¼ cup frozen corn kernels, ½ small green bell pepper, cut into thin strips, ½ small red bell pepper, cut into strips, 2 tbs Parmesan or Romano cheese.

STEP 1: Melt butter over medium heat in a large, deep skillet. Add the rice to the pan and let the rice toast (brown a bit), stirring frequently.

STEP 2: Add the chopped onion, shredded carrot and garlic to the pan. Fry for about 2 minutes, stirring constantly.

STEP 3: Stir in the water and the chicken base; bring to a boil. Cover and reduce heat to low and cook until liquid is almost absorbed.

STEP 4: Add the cheese, peas, corn and peppers; stir to combine. Cover and cook over low heat for 5 minutes.

Taco Pie

Author: God

Show

Ingredients: 1 lb very lean ground beef, 1 medium onion, chopped, 1 pkg taco seasoning, 1 large chipotle (in adobo), chopped very fine, 1 can (4 oz) chopped green chiles, ½ cup baking mix (i.e. Bisquick), 2 eggs, ¾ cup milk 2 tbs salsa (mild, medium or hot), 1½ cups shredded cheddar cheese.

STEP 1: Preheat oven to 400 degrees F.

STEP 2: Brown ground beef and onion in a large (10-inch) heavy *OVEN-SAFE* skillet; drain off any fat. Stir in taco seasoning.

STEP 3: Stir in chopped chipotle. Top with green chilies and ½ cup of the cheese.

STEP 4: In a small bowl, combine the baking mix, eggs, salsa and milk. Beat well to combine. Pour over meat mixture in skillet.

STEP 5: Bake for 25-30 minutes or until knife inserted at the center of the pie comes out clean.

STEP 6: Top with remaining cheese and bake an additional 5-10 minutes. Let stand 5 minutes before slicing.

Peperoni pizza

Author: God

Show

Ingredients: 1 recipe pizza dough, 12 slices pepperoni, ½ cup chopped sweet or hot Italian sausage, ¼ cup pizza sauce, 2 cups shredded mozzarella, ½ cup shredded asiago, ¼ cup grated Romano or Parmesan cheese, Torn fresh basil leaves (optional).

STEP 1: Preheat oven to 425 degrees F. You can use either a round pizza pan or a small baking sheet (one with sides). I usually use the rectangular baking sheet and line it with parchment.

STEP 2: Roll or shape pizza dough into a 12-inch circle or 9 x 13 (approximate) rectangle. Place in pan and roll outside edges inward about ½ inch to form a rim.

STEP 3: Spread pizza sauce over bottom of crust up to the rim. Evenly distribute pepperoni and chopped sausage.

STEP 4: Top with cheeses and bake for 12-15 minutes, or until crust is golden brown and cheese is melted and bubbly.

Tortelini al fomo

Author: God

Show

Ingredients: 1 pkg fresh tortellini, Italian sausage variety, ½ cup mushrooms, sliced, 1 tbs olive oil, Sauce: ½ cup cream, ½ cup milk, ½ cup shredded asiago cheese, ½ tsp salt, ¼ tsp pepper.

STEP 1: Cook tortellini per package directions. Drain and rinse; set aside.

STEP 2: Heat a large skillet over medium-high heat. Add oil; heat.

STEP 3: Add mushrooms and saute until they begin to brown.

STEP 4: Reduce heat to medium-low. Add the milk, cream, salt and pepper. Stir to combine.

STEP 5: Add the cheese and stir until it is melted. Add the tortellini back to the pan and heat through.

Pot Roast in Foil

Author: God

Show

Ingredients: 1-3-5 lb chuck roast either bone-in or boneless (about 1-inch thick), 2 cups baby carrots, 1 cup whole button mushrooms (optional), 3 stalks celery, cut into 3-4-inch pieces, 1 can (10.75 oz) cream of mushroom soup, low-sodium preferred, 4 medium potatoes, cut in half, 1 envelope dry mushroom gravy mix, 2 tbs water.

STEP 1: Preheat oven to 425 degrees F. Tear off a large piece of heavy-duty aluminum foil (about 30-inches) and place it on a large baking sheet (a 15- or 18-inch cookie sheet/jellyroll pan) so that equal amounts of the foil overhang the sides of the baking sheet.

STEP 2: In a small bowl, mix the soup and gravy mix; set aside.

STEP 3: Place the chuck roast in the center of the foil on prepared baking sheet. Spread soup mixture over the top of the roast.

STEP 4: Place the celery, potatoes, carrots and mushrooms (if using) on top of the roast. Sprinkle with the water.

STEP 5: Make foil pouch by bringing up the overhanging sides and sealing top and sides well. I always leave about an inch or two "cushion" on the top for steam expansion.

STEP 6: Roast for 60 minutes if the steak/roast is 1-inch or slightly less; 90 minutes if the roast/steak is thicker than 1-inch.

Luau Ribs

Author: God

Show

Ingredients: 2 racks (4 lb), baby back ribs or 4 lb regular spare ribs, salt, pepper. Sauce: 1 cup pureed peaches, ⅓ cup ketchup, ⅓ cup cider vinegar, 5 garlic cloves, minced, 2 tbs soy sauce, ½ cup brown sugar, 2 tsp ginger. SLOW COOKER

STEP 1: Preheat oven to 450 degrees F. Line a large baking pan with aluminum foil (for easy clean up).

STEP 2: Mix all sauce ingredients in a small bowl.

STEP 3: Cut ribs into serving portions, single ribs for regular spare ribs and 2 ribs for baby backs. Lay ribs in a single layer in prepared baking pan. Sprinkle with salt and pepper.

STEP 4: Bake at 450 degrees F for 15 minutes. Remove from oven, drain all liquid from the pan and reduce oven temp to 350 degrees F.

STEP 5: Pour sauce over ribs and cover pan with foil. Place in 350 degree oven and bake for 1½ hours, basting with sauce every 30 minutes. Remove foil cover during last 30 minutes of baking time.

STEP 6: Coat ribs with thickened sauce in the bottom of the pan and serve immediately.

STEP 7: Place all ingredients in slow cooker and cook for 6-8 hours on low. Sauce may be reduced in a saucepan on the stovetop if desired

Instant Pot Roast

Author: God

Show

Ingredients: 2-3 lb chuck roast, 1 cup rich beef stock, 1 envelope au jus gravy mix, small bay leaf, 1 bag (16 oz or so) frozen stew vegetables), To Thicken (if desired): ½ cup water, 2 tbs flour or cornstarch.

STEP 1: Trim as much excess fat off meat. Place in the Instant Pot.

STEP 2: Mix the stock and the au jus mix in a small bowl; pour over the meat. Add the bay leaf.

STEP 3: Seal pot and set on Meat for 55 minutes (this will be 10-15 psi or HI on other pots).

STEP 4: Let pressure reduce naturally for 10 minutes, then release the pressure completely.

STEP 5: You can remove the meat and keep it warm at this point--I do not.

STEP 6: Add the bag of vegetables and set the machine for low pressure for 5 minutes (or Vegetable setting on some machines)

Ceaser Chicken

Author: God

Show

Ingredients: 1 lb boneless skinless chicken thighs (or breasts/tenders), 1 cup creamy Caesar dressing, ½ cup sour cream, ½ cup grated Parmesan or Romano cheese, ½ cup shredded Parmesan cheese.

STEP 1: Preheat oven to 375℉.

STEP 2: In a small bowl, mix the dressing and sour cream; set aside.

STEP 3: Place chicken in a baking dish or oven-proof skillet big enough to hold all the chicken pieces. I used a 10-inch cast iron pan.

STEP 4: Spoon about 2 tbs of the dressing mixture on top of each chicken piece. Sprinkle the grated cheese evenly over all pieces. Then sprinkle the shredded cheese evenly over the pieces.

STEP 5: Bake for 20 minutes, or until chicken is 160℉ in the center of each pieces.

STEP 6: Set broiler to high and broil until the top is golden brown and bubbly.

Beefy bake

Author: God

Show

Ingredients: 1 lb very lean ground beef, 1 can (10.75 oz) tomato soup 1 can (15 oz) diced tomatoes, 1 can (4 oz) diced jalapenos (optional), ½ cup chopped onion, 2 cloves garlic, minced, ½ cup Mexican blend shredded cheese, 1 pkg (1.25 oz) taco seasoning (or 2 tbs), 2 tsp Adobo seasoning (Goya makes this), 8 oz (uncooked) rotini/rotelle pasta, Topping: 2-3 cups broken tortilla chips, 2 cups Mexican blend shredded cheese, ½ cup sliced black olives, 3 green onions, chopped.

STEP 1: Cook pasta per package directions. Drain and set aside.

STEP 2: Preheat oven to 350 degrees F. Lightly spray either and 11 x 7 or 13 x 9-inch baking pan.

STEP 3: Brown ground beef, onion and garlic in a large heavy skillet; drain fat.

STEP 4: Add the diced tomatoes, tomato soup, taco seasoning, Adobo seasoning, the ½ cup of cheese and the pasta. Stir to combine. Place mixture in the prepared baking pan.

STEP 5: Top with broken tortilla chips. Top tortilla chips with the 2 cups of cheese.

STEP 6: Bake at 350 degrees F for 25-30 minutes. Remove from oven and top with sliced olives and chopped green onion.
