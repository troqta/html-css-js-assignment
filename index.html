Spaghetti

Author: God

Show

Ingredients: sourdough bread 100g, crusts removed olive oil 4 tbsp chorizo 50g chunk, cut into tiny dice rosemary chopped to make 2 tsp wholewheat spaghetti 150g garlic 2 cloves, finely chopped dried chilli flakes ½ tsp parmesan 30g, finely grated Method

STEP 1: Put the sourdough in a food processor and whizz to rough breadcrumbs.

STEP 2: Heat 2 tbsp of olive oil in a non-stick frying pan and add the chorizo. Fry for 3 minutes then tip in the breadcrumbs and cook until crisp but not too dark. Stir in the rosemary and cook for a minute then take off the heat and season. Tip onto kitchen paper.

STEP 3: Cook the spaghetti following pack instructions. Heat another 2 tbsp of olive oil in a pan and sizzle the garlic and chilli for 2 minutes. Drain the pasta (keep a cup of the cooking water) then tip into the pan with the garlic-chilli oil and toss. Add a good splash of cooking water and the parmesan, and toss again. Serve sprinkled with the pangritata.

Cheesecake

Author: God

Show

Ingredients: 1 cup sliced almonds - coarsely choppped, 1 1/4 cup flour, 1/2 cup brown sugar, 1/2 cup butter, 1/2 cup sweetened shredded coconut, 2 (8 oz.) packages of cream cheese - softened 2/3 cups sugar, 2 eggs, 2 tsp. almond extract , 1 (21 oz.) cans cherry pie filling, 1/4 cup sweetened shredded coconut, 2 tbs sliced almonds, reserved crust,

Preparation: Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Prepare crust by mixing the almonds, flour, brown sugar and coconut. Cut in the shortening until mixture resembles small peas. Reserve 1/2 cup of crust for topping. Press remaining crust into a 11 x 7 inch pan. Bake for 10 minutes or until golden brown. Prepare filling by beating all ingredients together until smooth. Spread topping over partially-baked crust and bake for 15-20 minutes; remove from oven and spread the cherry pie filling over the topping. Sprinkle with reserved crumbs and additional coconut and almonds. Bake for 15-20 minutes. Remove from oven and cool completely. Refrigerate overnight. Makes 12 servings.

Catalina Wings

Author: God

Show

Ingredients: 3-4 lbs chicken wings, wing tip removed and disjointed, 2-3 tbs olive oil (only use if you grill or bake the wings). Sauce: ¾ cup California French (Catalina) dressing - OR- regular French dressing, 3 tbs brown sugar, 2 tbs soy sauce, 2 tbs ketchup, 2 tsp garlic powder, 1 tsp onion powder, ¼ tsp pepper,

STEP 1: In a small saucepan, mix all the sauce ingredients. Bring to boil over medium heat and cook for 2 minutes, stirring constantly. Remove from heat and set aside.

STEP 2: Toss the wings with the 2-3 tbs olive oil if grilling or baking

STEP 3: Cook wings either by grilling, baking, deep frying or slow cooking. I grilled mine on my gas grill and cooked them over medium-low heat for 40 minutes, turning once. Same time would apply to baking at 350 degrees F. Deep frying cook at 350 degrees F for 8-9 minutes. Slow cooking 4-6 hours on low.

STEP 4: Place cooked wings in a large bowl, pour sauce over them and toss to coat.

Bean Soup

Author: God

Show

Ingredients: 2 cups great northern beans, plus water to cover to soak, 3 carrots sliced thickly, ¼ cup chopped onion, 1 stalk celery, sliced, ½ tsp OR about 5 large fresh basil leaves, about 3 or 4 bunches celery leaves, 6 whole black peppercorns, 1 cup diced kielbasa (Polish sausage or smoked sausage), 1 cup cubed smoked ham, 4 cups vegetable stock.

STEP 1: Cover beans with water. Soak overnight. Drain beans.

STEP 2: Add beans to 5 quart slow cooker. Add all other ingredients. Cover and cook on HIGH for 2 hours. Reduce slow cooker heat to LOW and cook for 10-12 hours or until beans are very soft and soup looks thick and stew-like.

Macaroni salad

Author: God

Show

Ingredients: 2 cups elbow macaroni (uncooked), 2 stalks celery, finely diced, ¼ cup red onion, finely diced, ½ cup julienne (matchstick) carrots. Dressing: 1¼ cups mayonnaise, 2 tbs yellow mustard, 1 tsp salt, ¼ tsp garlic powde.

STEP 1: Cook elbows per package directions. Drain and rinse; place in a large bowl and set aside until cool.

STEP 2: Add the celery red onion and matchstick carrots to the cooked elbows; mix well.

STEP 3: In a small bowl, mix all the dressing ingredients.

STEP 4: Add dressing to elbows and gently mix to coat all the macaroni.

STEP 5: Cover and refrigerate for about 1 hour.

Grape Salad

Author: God

Show

Ingredients: 8 oz softened cream cheese, 1 cup sour cream, ⅓ cup sugar, 1 tbs fresh lemon juice, ½ tsp vanilla, 4 lbs seedless grapes (red, white or a combination of the two), ¼ cup chopped walnuts.

STEP 1: Beat cream cheese, sour cream and sugar until very smooth. Add the vanilla and lemon juice; beat at low speed to combine.

STEP 2: Remove grapes from the stem and place in a large bowl; stir in desired amount of dressing.

STEP 3: Place in a large serving bowl and garnish with chopped walnuts.

STEP 4: Serve immediately or cover and chill until ready to use.

Asian Spinach

Author: God

Show

Ingredients: 1 tbs oil, 2 cloves garlic, crushed, 1 bag (about 4-5 cups) baby spinach or regular spinach, washed and de-stemmed, ½-1 tsp coarse sea salt.

STEP 1: Heat oil and crushed garlic over high heat in large saucepan. Oil must be heated almost to the smoke point. Swirl pan constantly, and remove and discard garlic when it starts to turn golden.

STEP 2: Place spinach in hot oil and stir constantly, maybe for 1 minute. Add salt and stir. Spinach will wilt down to ½ the original amount.

Confetti Rice

Author: God

Show

Ingredients: 6 tbs butter, 1½ cups jasmine rice (uncooked), 1 tbs chicken base, 3 cups water, 1 small onion, chopped, ½ cup shredded carrot, 1 clove garlic, minced, ¼ cup frozen baby peas, ¼ cup frozen corn kernels, ½ small green bell pepper, cut into thin strips, ½ small red bell pepper, cut into strips, 2 tbs Parmesan or Romano cheese.

STEP 1: Melt butter over medium heat in a large, deep skillet. Add the rice to the pan and let the rice toast (brown a bit), stirring frequently.

STEP 2: Add the chopped onion, shredded carrot and garlic to the pan. Fry for about 2 minutes, stirring constantly.

STEP 3: Stir in the water and the chicken base; bring to a boil. Cover and reduce heat to low and cook until liquid is almost absorbed.

STEP 4: Add the cheese, peas, corn and peppers; stir to combine. Cover and cook over low heat for 5 minutes.
